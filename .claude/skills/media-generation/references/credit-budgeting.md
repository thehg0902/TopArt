# Credit Budgeting
Set a per-site credit cap at storyboard time (default proposal: enough
for planned assets + 30% iteration margin) and write it at the top of the
build's MEDIA_LOG table. Track actual credits per row; running total in
the table footer. At 80% of cap: warn. At cap: stop, present spend vs
plan, ask before continuing. Video >> image cost: storyboard images
first, confirm direction with the human on cheap assets before any video
generation.
