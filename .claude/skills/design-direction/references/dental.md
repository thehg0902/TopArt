# Niche Playbook: Dental / Clinics
Converts on: calm trust + easy booking. Visitors are often anxious.
Direction defaults: soft light palette (avoid pure clinical white-blue
cliche - warm neutrals + teal/sage accent work), rounded radii, real team
photos over stock smiles. Hero: static or very subtle motion; reassuring
headline. Must-have: book CTA (Calendly flag likely), services in plain
language (not jargon), team section with credentials, insurance/payment
info, reviews. Avoid: graphic procedure imagery, fear-based copy.
Must-have sections: hero, services, about (team), testimonials, faq (insurance/payment), contact (book CTA), cta
