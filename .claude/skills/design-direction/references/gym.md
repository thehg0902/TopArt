# Niche Playbook: Gym / Fitness Studio
Converts on: vibe match + trial offer + schedule clarity.
Direction defaults: high energy = bold condensed type, dark bg with one
electric accent; boutique/yoga = light, airy, calm palette. Real people
over equipment shots. Hero: training footage (motion sells energy).
Must-have: trial/first-class CTA, class schedule or hours, coach faces,
pricing transparency question to client (show vs inquire). Avoid: generic
stock bodybuilders, aggressive tone for wellness studios.
Must-have sections: hero, services (classes/schedule), about (coaches), testimonials, cta (trial offer), contact
