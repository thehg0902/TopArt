# Niche Playbook: Coffee Shop / Cafe
Converts on: atmosphere + location + hours. Users decide in seconds.
Direction defaults: warm neutrals or rich earth tones; one serif or
humanist display face for headings; generous whitespace; photography of
space/drinks/people over product grids. Hero: ambient video or warm
still of the space. Must-have sections: hours prominent (header + contact),
menu (PDF link or simple styled list - ask which), map, Instagram link.
Avoid: stock latte-art cliches, dark moody palettes for family cafes.
Conversion: directions + "order/menu" over forms.
Must-have sections: hero, menu, gallery, contact (hours + map + Instagram), cta
