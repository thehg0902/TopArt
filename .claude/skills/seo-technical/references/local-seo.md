# Local SEO On-Site Rules
NAP consistency is the highest-weight controllable: the exact string used
on GBP is the canonical form - copy it, don't normalize it.
City/area mentions: hero-adjacent copy, service sections, footer address,
title tags - naturally, ~once per surface. Service pages target
"{service} {city}" intent each.
Internal links: every service mentioned in copy links to its section/page.
Embed map on contact (maps-gbp skill) and link "Get directions" to the
GBP-linked Maps URL from client.md, not a fresh search URL.
