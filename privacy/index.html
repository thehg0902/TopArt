Privacy Policy

What we do with your information

Last updated: July 6, 2026

The short version

This website collects almost nothing. There are no analytics trackers, no advertising cookies, and the site stores nothing in your browser. The only information we receive is what you choose to send us.

The quote form

If you submit the quote request form, we receive your name, phone number, and whatever you write in the message. We use it to contact you about your roof — nothing else. (This policy will name the delivery provider when the form goes live.)

The map

The contact page embeds a Google Map of our location. Google may set its own cookies inside that embed under Google's privacy policy.

Phone calls

If you call us, that call is between you and us like any phone call. The website itself records nothing when you tap a phone link.

How long we keep it

We keep quote requests as long as needed to respond and follow up on the work, then as required for normal business records in Ontario, Canada.

Your requests

Want to know what we have from you, or want it deleted? Call us at (416) 930-8939 and we'll sort it out.
